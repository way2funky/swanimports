Water Products

Live by-pass for water mains

Mains Wizard

Based on the WASK 312 gas bypass system, Sarco Stopper leaders in line stopping technology and developed in partnership with Crane created the award winning Mains Wizard system. This system allows for under pressure bagging off of 3”-8” mains with by-pass to provide for uninterrupted supply to customers, drastically reducing water wastage (the mains does not require flushing), major cost savings (customer notifications, customer rebates) and no risk of knock on burst on recharge. The equipment is light weight with no power source required and can be comfortably used by a two man crew. Currently under development is the next version of the Mains Wizard which will allow for the bagging off of 9”-12” mains.

Live replacements for hydrants

Hydrant Wizard

Providing the ability to isolate 3” & 4” risers and mains without affecting the supply on the primary mains the Hydrant Wizard is the versatile solution for providing quick repairs, maintenance extensions and valve removals. As only the hydrant is isolated there is no depressurising the main, no sediment disturbance and no knock on effect. The Hydrant Wizard is easy to use, requires a small excavation area, can work up to 8” bar mains pressure and uses a reusable blade saddle for future access.

Gas Products

teeset drilling and tapping equipment

Wask 312

Allows for the drilling and tapping of gas mains along with a bagging off and by-pass system providing a safe and effective way of maintaining and extending gas mains. The equipment is suitable for use up to 2 bar(30psi) and will fit mains 3”-12”. A recent development has been the single hole bagging system (SBH) allowing for 2 bags to be deployed from a single hole reducing entry points, excavation size and safety benefits.

Ball Valve Products

Electrofusion Ball Valves

Valves for use within the gas industry made from PE 100 with full port design for high flow with a wide range of sizes (20mm to 400mm). Available for electrofusion, butt fusion or socket fusion and comply with standards EN1555-4, ISO90001, ASME B16:40. Valves also come with the option of single or double purge valves, extended stems and gearing. All valves are fully tested by Polytec and are well suited for extended life for below ground installation.

Compression Couplings

LA couplings (rubber ringed steel) allow for the connection of plain pipes from dissimilar materials (Steel, PVC & PE) and removes the need for the threading, grooving or welding of pipes. The design of these couplings from Riken Japan makes them highly resistant to expansion, contraction, vibration and deflection and can be used for gas, water, hot water, air and many other applications.
